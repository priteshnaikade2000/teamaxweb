About Us

Finding Inspiration in Every Turn

Welcome to TeaMax Cafe, the prestigious brand of the F&B Division within the renowned Ashoka Group, a prominent Indian conglomerate since 1969. With a diverse portfolio spanning across various sectors, including B2B and B2C segments, our headquarters are located in Hyderabad. As a trusted name in the industry, we excel in providing exceptional educational solutions, logistics and supply chain services, real estate development, retail product innovation, and, of course, the delightful world of food and beverages.

At TeaMax Cafe, we are committed to delivering an unparalleled dining experience to our valued customers. With our rich heritage and expertise, we have curated a wide array of delectable offerings that cater to diverse palates. Our menu reflects a perfect blend of innovation and tradition, continually evolving to introduce new flavors and recipes that will delight your taste buds.

Driven by our passion for excellence, we strive to create an inviting atmosphere where customers can relax and indulge in culinary delights. Whether you are seeking a cozy ambiance for a casual gathering or a vibrant space for a business meeting, TeaMax Cafe offers the perfect setting for every occasion.

Being part of the Ashoka Group, we embody the group's values of integrity, quality, and customer-centricity. We take pride in our commitment to delivering exceptional service and ensuring customer satisfaction. Our extensive presence across India allows us to cater to a diverse range of communities and share the joys of TeaMax Cafe with people from all walks of life. Come and experience the world-class hospitality and exquisite flavors that TeaMax Cafe has to offer. We invite you to join us on this remarkable culinary journey, where taste, innovation, and the Ashoka Group's legacy blend harmoniously.
